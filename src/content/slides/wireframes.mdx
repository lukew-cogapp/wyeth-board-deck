## Wireframing the key pages

<p class="opacity-85">We've prototyped the key page types as interactive wireframes, and used them to help model the website's structure and content with Laura and Will.</p>

<Bullets>
- **Faceted catalogue browse**, including a Pennsylvania/Maine place map and filters for medium, series, people and decade.
- **A working-process view** showing a piece from sketch to finished tempera.
- **A family timeline** across three generations of Wyeth artists.
- **Scholarly tools**: a term guide for attribution and dating, citations, and a clear image-rights page.
- **The full grants story**: guidelines, fellowships and the track record of past recipients.
</Bullets>
