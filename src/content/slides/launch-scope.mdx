# ~300 works at launch

<p class="lead opacity-85">From 7,000–11,000 records in the collection system.</p>

<p class="lead opacity-80">"Broader reach, deeper connection." Not mile wide, inch deep, but letting people go <strong>mile deep</strong> on a small number of works.<span class="cite">Laura West</span></p>

<p class="opacity-85">Depth could mean surfacing more than the finished work: studies, in-progress and detail images, conservation, the stories around each piece.</p>

<p class="opacity-85">A living document that grows over time, not an incomplete one. How we frame this matters.</p>
