## A perception gap, not a popularity gap

import Bullets from "../../components/Bullets.astro";
import Quote from "../../components/Quote.astro";

<Bullets size="lg">
- Crystal Bridges planned a Wyeth exhibition **without contacting WFAA**.
- An insurer reached out to Winterthur about a damaged work, **not the Foundation**.
- People routinely **confuse WFAA with the Brandywine Museum**.
- Wyeth's standing in art-historical discourse has **slipped since his mid-century peak**.
</Bullets>

<Quote cite="Will Coleman">We don't have a popularity problem. We have a perception problem.</Quote>
