## Open decisions for definition

import Bullets from "../../components/Bullets.astro";

<Bullets>
- **Naming.** Could just be the Wyeth CR, but a name could give it real identity. "Access" is taken; "Wyeth World" and "looking again" were floated
- **Content ownership.** Keeping the site fresh needs dedicated time. A sustainable plan for who feeds it, and how often
- **Data quality.** Quality assurance and validation plan for TMS data before launch
- **Launch messaging.** How to frame ~300 works without seeming incomplete
- **Public engagement.** Who owns the social side, and how it connects to the site (active 100k+ member groups already exist)
- **Search engine & AI discoverability.** Capture "American art", "Shaker design", not just "Wyeth"
</Bullets>
