## Authority

<Quote cite="From the future retrospective exercise">An invaluable resource: the most plentiful and most accurate information, more than any other source. The primary reference.</Quote>

<p class="opacity-80">The catalogue raisonné is the centrepiece. The ambition goes beyond a scholarly catalogue: process, conservation and context alongside the works, on the Access O'Keeffe model (the Georgia O'Keeffe Museum's online catalogue).</p>

<p class="opacity-80 mt-2"><strong>The catch is data quality.</strong> If we launch with visible gaps or errors, the authority claim falls apart. This needs a quality assurance plan well before launch.</p>
