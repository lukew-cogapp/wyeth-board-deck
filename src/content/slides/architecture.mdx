## Build for the seven-year vision

<Bullets size="lg">
- **Catalogues for other Wyeth artists** (out of scope now, but the architecture should anticipate them)
- **Oral histories, video, new essays commissioned from scholars.** Some audio and video already exists
- **Links from each work out** to the Kuerner farm, to Maine, to interviews, to conservation stories
</Bullets>

<Quote cite="Noted during the board discussion">From the CR there should be a maximum number of out paths: to the Kuerner farm, to the valley in Maine that's the subject of a painting, to an interview with a farmer. Don't build an architecture that won't support that.</Quote>
