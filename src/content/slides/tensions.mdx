## Tensions to resolve

import Bullets from "../../components/Bullets.astro";

<p class="opacity-85">Not problems to fix. Competing pulls the design has to hold in balance.</p>

<Bullets>
- **Professional vs public.** A serious working tool and a way in, without watering down either
- **Depth vs breadth.** ~300 works should feel substantial, a living catalogue rather than an incomplete one
- **WFAA vs Brandywine.** Make clear WFAA is an independent foundation, not a museum
- **Open access vs forgery risk.** Some works get deep zoom; most stay presentation-grade, detailed enough for study but not for faithful reproduction
</Bullets>
