## Self-service as the operating model

<Bullets size="lg">
- **Scholars** find and cite images themselves, without institutional gatekeepers.
- **Curators** check object locations without emailing staff.
- **Grantees** understand whether and how to apply.
- **The grants programme grows**: guidelines, deadlines and outcomes easy to find.
</Bullets>

<p class="opacity-85">Good self-service serves users and frees up staff time. It came up in every session.</p>
